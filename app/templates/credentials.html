About Secure Credentials

Security & Privacy

Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely. They are never sent to the AI model. When you reference a credential in an automation prompt using {{credential_name}}, only the placeholder is sent to the AI. The actual value is injected only when the browser needs it.

How to use credentials in automations:

  1. Store your credential below (e.g., name: gmail_password)
  2. In your automation prompt, use {{gmail_password}} as a placeholder
  3. The automation will securely use your credential without exposing it to the AI

Example: Instead of "Log into Gmail with password MySecret123", use "Log into Gmail with password {{gmail_password}}"
